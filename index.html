Real Taste With Real Power

Dairy Power Ltd. deals in milk and milk products under our "Dairy Power" brand with our motto 'Real Power with Real Taste' since year 2008. Dairy Power Ltd. is established as a partnership firm, registered under the Indian Partnership Act,1932.

Malai Lassi

Dairy Power Malai Lassi is a rich, creamy, and refreshing drink made with the goodness of pure curd. Packed with natural probiotics and essential nutrients, it aids in digestion and provides prebiotics. Perfect for cool down your day, this nutritional drink blends perfectly to chill feeling and wellness.

Dahi

Dairy Power Dahi is thick, creamy, and made from the finest quality milk for a rich taste. Carefully fermented, it is packed with probiotics that support digestive health and boost immunity. Ideal for meals, cooking, and nutrition to your everyday diet.

Paneer

Dairy Power Paneer is soft, fresh, and made from pure milk using traditional methods. Rich in protein, calcium, and nutrients, it's a healthy choice for your daily diet. Perfect for cooking a variety of delicious dishes, our paneer enhances the King taste.
