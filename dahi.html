Our Malai Dahi is crafted from the finest quality, full-cream milk and set using a time-honoured process that ensures a thick, luscious, and perfectly consistent curd. Every spoonful is a delight, with a velvety smooth texture and a mild, pleasant tang that speaks of its natural purity.

Indulge in a dessert that's truly worthy of celebration. Our Royal Kesar Shrikhand is a luxurious delicacy crafted from thick, creamy hung curd (chakka), strained to perfection. This rich base is then sweetened and delicately infused with the finest strands of pure saffron (Kesar) and aromatic cardamom.

Quench your thirst with nature's ultimate cooling refreshment. Our Masala Buttermilk is made by churning our fresh dahi to a light, smooth consistency, creating the perfect base for a revitalizing drink.
